No account. No password. Read this once — it's short.

What we store
◉Display name — what your friends see. You set it.
◷Timezone + sleep schedule — so we know when to hold pings.
⌁Push endpoint — a token your browser generates. We use it to deliver notifications. We never see your device ID.
◌Last active date — an ambient signal for your friends' cards, not a log.
What we never store
✕No email or phone number
✕No message content — pings are routing signals, not messages
✕No call transcripts — only "you last connected on [date]"
✕No analytics, no ads, no third-party SDKs
If you leave
↩Delete the app → push subscription expires automatically.
✕Settings → Delete my data removes everything instantly. Irreversible.
